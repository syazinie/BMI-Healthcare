About BMI Healthcare

BMI healthcare is an application that allows everybody either children, young or senior citizens,to find out about their own body mass index (BMI). BMI is the measurement between someone’s weight and height where it can be calculated by a person's weight in kilograms divided by the square of height in metres. Basically our application will calculate the BMI for you if you entered your weight and height. In addition, our application will show users their BMI’s categories as well. BMI is a necessary thing that should be known by all of us because from that we get to know whether our BMI range is suitable with our weight and height or not. In short words, it’s good or bad BMI. Then, with the categories that are being shown in our application after BMI results appear, people can take the correct steps to improve their BMI’s results in future.
